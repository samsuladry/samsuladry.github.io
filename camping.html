Campsite

The best campsite in the world.

It’s time to go back to basics and ditch our smartphones and gadgets, away from the comfort of the city life. After all, nothing is more exciting than wandering off to an unknown path and discovering new things!

Some of campsite in Malaysia:

Congkak River

Sungai Congkak Recreational Forest in Hulu Langat is an ideal gateway for those who want to experience the great Malaysian outdoors. Located 33km away from the city, it nestles beside orchards and tropical rainforests, with facilities available for camping, picnicking and swimming.

Kenong Rimba

A Scenic VaIIey Traversed By Rippling Mountains Kenong Rimba Park (rimba means jungle in Malay) is located at the fringe of Taman Negara on its eastern and northern boundaries. This green precinct covers a sprawling 128 square kilometres of Pahang‘s tropical rainforest.

Tadom Hill Resort

Tadom Hill Resort boasts almost 40 acres of limestones hills, lakes and trees, Boasting many exciting features and activities, this place is perfect for those who want to escape from the hustle and bustle of city life and unwind.
